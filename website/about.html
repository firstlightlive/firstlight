HOW THE STAKES WORK — NO FINE PRINT
The rule is simple: I post daily proof on Instagram. If a day is missed — no post by midnight — the first person to claim it gets ₹15,000 via direct UPI transfer within 24 hours. No excuses, no delays, no conditions.

Two ways to claim:
1. Comment on the missed day's Instagram post — first comment wins.
2. Comment on firstlight.live — the community section on this website counts too.

Why should you believe this? Because the proof is public. Every day is logged with timestamps — sleep data from my watch, run data from Strava, gym check-in, food code. The entire history is on the proof archive. You can verify any day, any metric.

This isn't a marketing stunt. There's no product to sell, no course, no coaching. This is a personal accountability system with real financial consequences. The money is mine. The risk is mine. Your only role is to watch — or collect.
THE IRON RULES — ZERO EXCEPTIONS
01 No rain stops me. I run in the rain. I've done it. Proof exists.
02 No storm stops me. Weather is not a variable. The run happens.
03 No city stops me. Different city, different timezone — same 5 km. Airport, hotel corridor, parking lot. Doesn't matter.
04 No flight stops me. If my flight departs between 00:30 AM and 6:00 AM — upon landing, I walk 5 km from the airport. GPS verified. No cab until the walk is done. Post may be delayed, but the distance never is.

06 5 km daily. Non-negotiable. Run or walk. Track or road. Home or airport. Race course or parking lot. The distance gets done. Every. Single. Day.
